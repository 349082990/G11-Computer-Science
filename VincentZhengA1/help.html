Background Information

The year is 2045, and computer technology has come a long way since you were in Grade 11. After years of working as a highly successful software developer, you've decided to pursue your true calling as a digital masseuse, bringing pain relief to humans and animals. Your success is solely determined by your brain... and your clicking finger.

Massage Mayhem Upgrades

  • Extra Muscular Fingers -- The first upgrade costs $100. Each additional muscle upgrade costs 10% more than the previous price, rounded up to the nearest cent (2 decimal places). Each muscular finger upgrade adds $5 to each click.
    • If the user has more than 5 Extra Muscular Finger upgrades, the client will be hurt 10% of the time. This will result in a loss of $100 on that click. 
    • You can only have 10 Extra Muscular Finger upgrades.
  • Incredibly Attractive Assistant -- The first upgrade costs $400. Each subsequent extraordinarily appealing assistance costs an additional 15% of the preceding amount, rounded to the closest penny (2 decimal places). Each helper contributes an additional $1 every second.
  • Ambidextrous -- This upgrade can only be accepted once. After taking into account the Extra Muscular Fingers bonus, it doubles the amount of money won every click.
  • Franchise -- The initial cost of the upgrade is $5000. Each successive franchise costs 10% more than the preceding one, rounded up to the closest penny (2 decimal places). Each franchise contributes an additional $200 every second. Each additional franchise provides just 95% of what the preceding franchise provided. So the first yields $200, the second yields $200x0.95, the third yields $200x0.95 2, and so on.
  • Franchisee Revolt -- After acquiring more than 8 franchises, there is a 15% chance of a franchise rebellion every 5 minutes. During a franchisee revolt, the user has the option of accepting Reduce Franchise Royalties (a 10% reduction in the amount each franchise gives, which is cumulative with each Franchisee Revolt), OR losing HALF of their franchises.
  • Initial Public Offering -- This is only offered after the player has played for ten minutes, and it can only be accepted once. If activated, the user receives $50,000 right away.
    • Corporate Power Struggle -- If a user does not click for more than 30 seconds after triggering the Initial Public Offering, the player is kicked out of the game. The visitor navigates to a website that displays an image of Elon Musk declaring that he pwns you.
  • Relocate -- Each time this is done, it costs $100,000. With each migration, the crime rate is reduced by 20%. It builds up over time. There will be a 0.25 x (0.80) probability of being robbed after the first relocation, and a 0.25 x (0.80) 2 chance of being robbed after the second relocation, and so on.
  • Hire Security -- This will cost you $5 every second. It decreases the amount of money robbed by half if it occurs. Each extra security measure continues to cost $5 per second. The effects are cumulative, which means they cut the quantity lost by 50%. So, after employing one security guard, the first loss due to robbery would be 50% x 50% = 25%. The addition of a second security guard would cut robbery losses by 0.50 x (0.50) ^2, or 12.5%.
